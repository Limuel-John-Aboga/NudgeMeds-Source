Configuring the WiFi system:



1. On the home page, click on the "Configure WiFi".


2. Once there, an input system will show, for SSID, type your desired WiFi name. (ex. SmartBox)


3. For password, you may type any desired password, you may use any complex password.


4. Once all filled, click on the submit button.


5. Lastly, for changes to take effect, you may unplug and plug again the device, causing a reset.



Setting the schedules, and starting the device:



1. Before everything, make sure that you've read or know the details on how to refill the device. If not, please refer to the device manual provided.


2. On the home page, click on the "Configure and start device".


3. Before and foremost, you must be notified that schedules must be set at least, 1 hour before the desired schedule. (ex. You may set the device at 6:00 am - 8:00 am, if the schedules desired are between 9:00 am - 7:00 am.)


4. Remember that the dispensation per day always starts from right to left, and dispensation per given schedule is from bottom to top.


5. Once there, a fill up form will be shown, where the user can fill up all the schedules desired.


6. After filling all the schedules up, click on the submit button.


7. Finally, close the website, and just kindly wait for your alarm to drink the medicine dispensed.



Updating the device:



Please check out the "update" link on home.
